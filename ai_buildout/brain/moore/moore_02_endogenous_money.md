## Working Capital Finance: The Core Mechanism

```
    THE MONETARY CIRCUIT
    ════════════════════
    
    Time: t                  Time: t+Δt
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    
    Firms decide to          Production occurs
    increase output          Goods produced but
         │                   not yet sold
         │                        │
         ↓                        ↓
    Need working capital    Inventories accumulate
         │                        │
         ↓                        ↓
    Borrow from banks       Sales occur
         │                        │
         ↓                        ↓
    +Loans                  Revenue received
         ║                        │
         ║ (creates)              ↓
         ↓                   Loans repaid
    +Deposits                     │
         │                        ↓
         │                   -Loans, -Deposits
         ↓                        │
    Wage bill paid               ↓
    Materials purchased      (Circuit closes)
    
    
    KEY EQUATION:
    ───────────
    
    ΔLoans ≈ Δ(Wage Bill) + Δ(Materials) + Δ(Inventories)
    
    ∴ ΔM = ΔLoans ≈ Δ(W·L) + Δ(Materials) + Δ(I_inventory)
    
    Money supply RESPONDS to production decisions
    Money supply does NOT cause production decisions
```

```
    QUANTITY THEORY (WRONG)          COST-PUSH THEORY (CORRECT)
    ═══════════════════════          ══════════════════════════
    
    M↑ (exogenous)                   W↑ faster than productivity
     │                                │
     │ "Too much money                │ Firms markup over
     │  chasing goods"                │ unit labor costs
     ↓                                ↓
    P↑                               P↑
                                      │
                                      │ Maintains real wages
                                      │ Higher nominal incomes
                                      ↓
                                     Demand for credit↑
                                      │
                                      │ Banks accommodate
                                      ↓
                                     M↑ (endogenous)
    
    Direction: M → P                 Direction: W → P → M
    
    
    INFLATION EQUATION (Post-Keynesian):
    
        π = Ẇ/W - Ȧ/A
        
    where π = inflation rate
          Ẇ/W = wage growth rate
          Ȧ/A = productivity growth rate

```

      • Credit (income)
      • Collateral (wealth)
      • Character (history)

    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                         ↑
                                         │ Ceiling
    Currently drawn: $40,000             │
    ═══════════════════════              │
                                         │

    ─────────────────────────────────────┘
